Phuket, Thailand

Phuket is famous for its picturesque beaches, lively nightlife, and exceptional diving opportunities, attracting travelers from all over the world to experience its natural beauty and rich culture.

Here's Why We Recommend Phuket For You:
1- Your departure city is Melbourne and Phuket is not far from Melbourne.
2- You are looking for nightlife and Phuket has popular nightlife spots including Patong Beach, Bangla Road, and various beach clubs.

How to enjoy

Phuket, Thailand, offers a wide range of activities for travelers due to its beautiful landscapes, rich cultural history, and lively nightlife. Here are a few things a traveler can do:

Beaches: Phuket is renowned for its stunning beaches. Patong Beach is the most popular and offers numerous water sports, but there are also quieter options like Kata Beach or Karon Beach.

Old Phuket Town: Explore the Sino-Portuguese architecture, colorful buildings, local markets, and quaint cafes in Phuket's Old Town.

Big Buddha: Visit the 45-meter-tall Big Buddha statue located on Nakkerd Hill. It offers an impressive view of the island.

Wat Chalong: This is the most important of the 29 Buddhist temples of Phuket. It's beautifully decorated with countless reflections from hundreds of tiny glass pieces and several pagodas.

Phi Phi Islands: Take a boat tour to the Phi Phi Islands, known for their stunning beaches and excellent diving and snorkeling opportunities.

Phang Nga Bay: Known for its sheer limestone karsts that jut vertically out of the emerald-green water, Phang Nga Bay is a must-visit spot. You can explore it by kayak or boat tour.
